Your personal closet — organized like a dashboard

Digital Wardrobe helps you store clothing photos, save details (category, color, tags), and track how often you wear items — so planning outfits gets easier over time.

Add Clothing Item View Wardrobe

Built as a student project using Supabase + Cloudinary. Works best with Live Server.

Key Features

Add Items with Photos
Upload clothing images and store category, color, and tags.
Wardrobe Grid
Browse your collection in a clean, responsive card grid.
Wear Tracking
Tap “Mark as Worn” to increase wear count and see top items.

How It Works

Step-by-step

  • Open Add Item and upload a photo.
  • Choose category + enter color and tags.
  • The image is stored in Cloudinary and the item details go to Supabase.
  • In Wardrobe, view items and mark them as worn.

Designed for

  • Students building a portfolio project
  • Anyone who wants quick outfit organization
  • Future upgrades: filters, search, analytics, calendar
